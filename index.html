About the Chiron Super Sport

The Bugatti Chiron Super Sport isn’t just fast — it's legendary. With 1,578 horsepower and a top speed of 273 mph, it's one of the fastest production cars on the planet.

Sculpted with aerospace precision and powered by a beastly quad-turbo W16, the Chiron Super Sport is a masterpiece of engineering and luxury — built for those who demand the absolute best.
